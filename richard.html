Will Richard
This is Will Richard. He was born on December 24th, 2002 in Fairburn, Georgia. He attended Woodward Academy and after high school committed to play basketball for the Belmont Bruins. In his first season with the Bruins he put up some notable performances after entering the transfer portal. He transferred to play with the Gators for the 2022-2023 season. After many more outstanding performances the most important of all was his 18 points including his 4 threes in the National Championship game in 2025.
Some of Richard's accomplishments include :
  • OVC All-Newcomer team (2022)
  • SEC Tournament Title (2025)
  • NCAA Tournaments Title(2025)
