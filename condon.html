Alex Condon
This is Alex Condon. He was born on July 25th, 2004 in Perth, Western Australia. He initally attended Hale School before transferring over to the Basketball Australia Centre for Excellence (CoE) at the Australian Institute of Sport. He played two seaons for the men's team in the NBL1. Then he signed to play for the Florida Gators in the 2023-2024 season.
Some of Condon's accomplishments include :
  • SEC-All Freshmen Team(2024)
  • SEC Tournament Title (2025)
  • NCAA Tournaments Title(2025)
  • Third-Team ALL-SEC Selection(2025)
  • SEC Player of the Week 3x(2025)
